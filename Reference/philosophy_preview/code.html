Dharma

Dharma is the foundational principle of existence, representing righteous duty, cosmic order, and the moral path that sustains the soul and the universe.

Tapas

Disciplined heat and spiritual effort. Tapas is the inner fire generated through practice that purifies the mind and strengthens the will against distractions.

Indriya Nigraha

The mastery of the senses. By withdrawing the senses from external objects, one attains the stability of mind required for deep meditation and self-realization.

"When the senses are controlled, the mind is at peace."

— Bhagavad Gita
