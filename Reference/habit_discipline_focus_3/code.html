Teachings from the Bhagavad Gita for daily discipline.

What Krishna teaches today

"tasmād yasya mahā-bāho nigṛhītāni sarvaśaḥ indriyāṇīndriyārthebhyas tasya prajñā pratiṣṭhitā"

Therefore, O mighty-armed, his knowledge is firmly established whose senses are completely restrained from their objects.

The Practice

In moments of impulse, pause before action.
